About the Book

Wok This Way delivers flavor-forward, keto-friendly recipes inspired by East Asian kitchens—brothy ramens, hotpots, dumplings, and umami-rich sauces. Practical swaps and clear techniques make low-carb cooking approachable for everyday life.

  • Brothy and dry-style keto ramen
  • Dumplings using napa or pak choi wrappers
  • Sauces and sides that boost flavor
  • Smart swaps that keep carbs low
  • Practical guidance for keto and intermittent fasting

No rice. No guilt. Just good food.
